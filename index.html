1. Click on the large + above to create a new list category.

2. Click on a Category's + on the right to create a new list item.

3. Select a category or list item and start typing to edit it.

4. Click the gear icon to access the settings for the category/list item:
- Trashcan deletes the item.
- Click and hold the Up/Down Arrow to reorder.
- Paint palette changes the background color of the category/list item.

5. Click the Menu Icon on the left to open or close the list items.
